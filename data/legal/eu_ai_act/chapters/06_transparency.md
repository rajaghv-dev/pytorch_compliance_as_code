# CHAPTER V - TRANSPARENCY OBLIGATIONS FOR CERTAIN AI SYSTEMS

## Article 50 - Transparency obligations

1. Providers shall ensure that AI systems intended to interact with natural persons are designed and developed in such a way that the person is informed that they are interacting with an AI system, unless this is obvious from the circumstances.

2. Providers of AI systems that generate audio, image, video or text content shall ensure that the outputs are marked in a machine-readable format and are detectable as artificially generated or manipulated.

3. Deployers of an emotion recognition system or a biometric categorisation system shall inform the persons exposed to the system.

4. The obligations in this Article shall not apply to AI systems authorised by law enforcement authorities.
