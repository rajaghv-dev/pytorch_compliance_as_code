# CHAPTER VI - MEASURES IN SUPPORT OF INNOVATION

## Article 56 - Regulatory sandboxes

1. Member States shall ensure that national competent authorities establish AI regulatory sandboxes.

2. The AI regulatory sandbox shall be a controlled environment that facilitates the development, testing and validation of innovative AI systems for a limited time.

3. Participation in the sandbox shall be open to providers and deployers of AI systems.

4. During sandbox testing, the requirements under this Regulation may be temporarily relaxed.

5. The sandbox shall have clear conditions and monitoring mechanisms.

6. National competent authorities shall designate a sandbox authority.

---

## Article 57 - Support for regulatory sandboxes

The Commission shall support the establishment and operation of AI regulatory sandboxes.

---

## Article 58 - Transparency requirements

Innovative AI systems developed within a regulatory sandbox shall comply with transparency requirements.
