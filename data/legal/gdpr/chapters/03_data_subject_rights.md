# GDPR - CHAPTER 3 - RIGHTS OF THE DATA SUBJECT

## Article 12 - Transparent information and communication

The controller shall provide information to the data subject in a concise, transparent, and accessible form.

---

## Article 13 - Information to be provided

When data are collected, the controller shall provide:

(a) identity and contact details;

(b) purposes of processing;

(c) categories of data;

(d) recipients;

(e) retention period;

(f) rights to access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection;

(g) right to withdraw consent;

(h) right to lodge a complaint;

(i) source of data;

(j) automated decision-making.

---

## Article 14 - Information to be provided where data are not obtained from the data subject

When data are not obtained from the data subject, the controller shall provide the same information within one month.

---

## Article 15 - Right of access

The data subject shall have the right to obtain confirmation of whether personal data are being processed.

The data subject shall have the right to obtain a copy of the personal data, free of charge.

---

## Article 16 - Right to rectification

The data subject shall have the right to have inaccurate personal data rectified.

The data subject shall have the right to have incomplete personal data completed.

---

## Article 17 - Right to erasure ('right to be forgotten')

The data subject shall have the right to have personal data erased where:

(a) the data are no longer necessary;

(b) the data subject withdraws consent;

(c) the data subject objects to processing;

(d) the data have been unlawfully processed;

(e) the data have to be erased for compliance with a legal obligation.

---

## Article 18 - Right to restriction of processing

The data subject shall have the right to restriction of processing where:

(a) the accuracy of the data is contested;

(b) the processing is unlawful but the data subject opposes erasure;

(c) the controller no longer needs the data but the data subject needs them for legal claims;

(d) the data subject has objected to processing pending verification.

---

## Article 19 - Notification obligation regarding rectification or erasure

The controller shall communicate any rectification or erasure of personal data to each recipient to whom the data have been disclosed.

---

## Article 20 - Right to data portability

The data subject shall have the right to receive personal data in a structured, commonly used and machine-readable format.

The data subject shall have the right to transfer those data to another controller.

---

## Article 21 - Right to object

The data subject shall have the right to object to processing based on legitimate interests or public interest.

The controller shall cease processing unless it demonstrates compelling legitimate grounds.

---

## Article 22 - Automated individual decision-making

The data subject shall have the right not to be subject to decisions based solely on automated processing which produce legal effects.

This includes profiling.

Exceptions apply where:

(a) necessary for a contract;

(b) authorized by Union or Member State law;

(c) based on explicit consent.

---

## Article 23 - Restrictions

Union or Member State law may restrict the rights in this Chapter where necessary for:

(a) national security;

(b) defence;

(c) criminal enforcement;

(d) important public interest;

(e) legal claims.
